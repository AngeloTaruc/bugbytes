About Us

About HANA Shelter

HANA Shelter, formerly known as SANA (Saving Abused and Neglected Animals), was founded by Ernie Garcia in April 2020. Amidst the pandemic season, Ernie, a solo rescuer, recognized the urgent need to create a safe haven for stray animals. Starting with 30 rescued dogs kept at his personal home, HANA has now grown into a sanctuary for nearly 200 rescued dogs.

Located on a 500 sq.m lot in Baras, Rizal, HANA Shelter operates with a dedicated team. In addition to Ernie, there are 7 other administrators and 5 caretakers onsite, all working tirelessly to manage the shelter’s daily operations.


Our Vision

To be a self-sustaining animal shelter that will continue to provide adequate care to rescued animals.

Our Mission

Rescue, Rehabilitate, Rehome, Educate.

HANA Shelter Commitments

Feeding and Care

HANA rescues receive proper care and nutrition. They are fed twice a day with a balanced diet that includes rice, dog food, sawdust, meat, and vegetables.

Health Initiatives

Annually, HANA conducts Spay and Neuter programs and Mass Vaccination projects to ensure the well-being of its furry residents.

Community Support

HANA sustains itself entirely through the generous donations of its supporters. To raise funds, the shelter engages in online merchandise sales and collaborates with other organizations for income opportunities.

Education and Advocacy

As strong advocates for animal welfare, HANA actively participates in speaking engagements to promote responsible pet ownership and animal rights.

HANA Shelter Team

Our dedicated team consists of - administrators and - onsite caretakers, all working tirelessly to ensure the smooth operation of our shelter and the well-being of our animal residents.

TOGETHER WE CAN MAKE
A DIFFERENCE

Your generosity is the heartbeat of HANA Shelter. As we rely solely on your donations, every contribution directly supports our mission to provide a safe haven for those in need. Together, we can offer hope, healing, and a brighter future for our community.

The heart of our mission to provide love, care, and support to animals in need. Your generosity fuels our efforts to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome countless animals, ensuring they have the chance to thrive and find their forever homes.

Every donation, no matter the size, makes a significant impact. Your support enables us to provide essential veterinary care, nutritious food, comfortable shelter, and enrichment activities for our animals. It also helps us expand our reach, raise awareness, and advocate for animal welfare on a broader scale. Whether you choose to make a in kind or monetary donation, your kindness reverberates far beyond our walls, touching the lives of animals and humans alike.
